Situated in the heart of the Norfolk countryside equidistant East Dereham and Swaffham,
The Partridge offers spacious, comfortable, well equipped en-suite accommodation.  

Our contemporary restaurant serves delicious traditional and continental dishes using local produce and 
specialises in coeliac, diabetics, lactose intolerant, vegetarians, vegans and all other dietary requirements.  
We only use fresh ingredients so dishes can be adapted to suit your needs.

Private fishing lakes with 9 acres of wild flower meadows for you to explore the wildlife and their habitat.   

Our indoor heated swimming pool for all guests use with a sauna and jacuzzi to relax in, late evening or early morning.
